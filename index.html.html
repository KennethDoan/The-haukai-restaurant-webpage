Kia Ora!

We are located in the mighty bay of plenty. With plenty of things and places to visit around this beautiful area, all the exploring will leave you hungry. This is where we come in, mixing Maori and Pakeha cuisine we are sure we have the menu to feed you and the family.

About
Us

We started this business with the aim of spreading the culture and food of Aoteroa. We specialise in Maori cuisine fused together from fresh locally sourced ingredients. Wether you're new to the area or a local, feel free to have a chat with us about the history of the area.
